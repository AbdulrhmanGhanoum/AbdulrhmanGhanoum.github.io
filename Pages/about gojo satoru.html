Gojo Satoru in Jujutsu Kaisen Anime

Satoru Gojo (五条悟 Gojō Satoru) is one of the main protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world. Satoru is the pride of the Gojo Clan, the first person to inherit both the Limitless and the Six Eyes in four hundred years. He works as a teacher at the Tokyo Jujutsu High and uses his influence to protect and train strong young allies.

Immeasurable Cursed Energy

Satoru Gojo is known in the jujutsu community as the most powerful jujutsu wizard in the world. He earned this nickname due to the immense amount of cursed energy he possesses. His cursed energy is great enough to use an AoE Expansion at least five times in a day, whereas most mages can only use it once. Gojo has perfect control over his cursed energy, can activate Area of Effect in a second, use reverse cursed technique, and even combine positive and negative cursed energy. Gojo is also known to have two overarching abilities; The first person in four hundred years to possess both the Six Eyes and the Gojo family's Unlimited cursed technique.

Gojo Satoru vs Toji

After Toji stabbed Satoru in the neck, believing he had successfully killed Gojo, Gojo returns in a state of madness, having been just moments away from death. They resume their fight once again in episode 8 of the second season.

The relationship between Gojo & Geto

During a period known as their blue spring of youth, Geto and Gojo attended Tokyo Jujutsu High together. Despite their conflicting philosophies, they became best friends and often fought together in battle.
